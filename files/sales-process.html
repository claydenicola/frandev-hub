FranDev Sales Process

Operation Lunar Boutique 🚀

Five stages, one question each — how leads move through the pipeline from first contact to signed franchisee.

Overview Intake Qualifying Closing Signed Dead Lead / Nurture

Sales Process Buckets

Every lead sits in one of five buckets. Each bucket answers exactly one question.

BucketThe only question it should answer
IntakeIs this a real prospect worth our time?
QualifyingCan this person fund, open, and operate successfully?
ClosingShould we formally award them a franchise?
SignedHas the deal been executed and handed off cleanly?
Dead Lead / NurtureIs this recoverable later, or truly lost?
1

Intake

First contact and basic verification.

Intake Checklist

  • Form submission and automated welcome sequence complete (email + SMS with Path to Ownership doc, Mac's video, Calendly link)
  • Introductory call conducted
  • FDD sent to lead through Portal
  • Application sent to lead
  • FDD Receipt received (signed by lead via portal)
All boxes checked→Lead moves into the Qualifying stage
2

Qualifying

Can this candidate fund, open, and operate successfully?

Qualifying Checklist

  • FDD review completed and clarifying questions answered
  • Application completed — minimums met, realistic funding path, no red flags
  • 3Q question write-up completed
  • Interview assessments passed
  • Realistic expectations confirmed by lead
  • Next meeting scheduled
All boxes checked→Lead moves into the Closing stage
3

Closing

Eliminate risk, secure approval, and formally award the candidate.

Closing Checklist

  • Funding validated — pre-approval or proof of funds confirming $250k–$400k liquid/accessible
  • Validation requests accommodated; no remaining concerns from lead
  • FranDev presentation made to Management and formal approval to award gained
  • Franchise awarded (award communication sent) and accepted by lead
  • FDD 16-day compliance confirmed
  • Agreements drafted and sent with execution instructions
All boxes checked→Lead moves into the Signed stage
4

Signed / Handoff

Execute the deal, collect funds, and hand the franchisee off to onboarding.

Signed / Handoff Checklist

  • Franchise agreement fully executed by all parties
  • Initial franchise fee collected and confirmed by finance
  • Franchisee profile created in CRM / FranConnect
  • Sales handoff memo completed
  • Onboarding kickoff scheduled
  • Handoff accepted by onboarding team
All boxes checked→Handoff complete
5

Dead Lead / Nurture

Select one primary category, then a sub-reason.

Inactive but Nurture

Potential to revive

Sub-reasons
  • Stalled / timing
  • Lost contact
  • Lost interest
  • Never made contact
  • Unqualified for now
  • Other (note required)

Dead Forever

No future pursuit

Sub-reasons
  • Spam
  • Permanently unqualified
  • Bad fit forever (personality / values)
  • Bought different franchise
  • Other (note required)
